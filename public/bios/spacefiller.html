Algorithmic Art Assembly
March 10–11–12 2022
Gray Area, San Francisco

SPACEFILLER w/ Mick Marchan

SPACEFILLER consists of artists Alexander Nagy and Alexander Miller, and is the result of their long time collaboration and pursuit of visual art, music, programming and math. They reside in Seattle, WA. Through the use of generative algorithmic graphics, projection mapping and tactile interaction, SPACEFILLER seeks to establish venues for experimentation, play and discovery. By allowing viewers to manipulate the parameters of simulated natural systems, they create an opportunity to explore the delicate balance between order, chaos and randomness. For their AAA2020 slot, they’ll be presenting an audiovisual piece they’ve been working on with computer music / sound artist Mick Marchan.
