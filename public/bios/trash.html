Trash Panda QC

Trash Panda QC is a producer, dj, and live performer based in NYC. While taking influence from classic rave tunes, noise, and footwork, his music is firmly set on leaving history behind for the excitement of the unknown, preferably with a lot of sub bass melting through constantly-shifting rhythms. Past releases and remixes have appeared on Moss Archive, Conditional, Tsuku Boshi, and Co-Dependent, as well as an extensive self-released discography. In the past two years of quarantine, Trash Panda QC's activities have expanded online, including the Casual Internet Party series of curated live streams, a spatialized webVR sound environment for Currents.fm's Common Festival, and Is Under Location Surfaces, a double album and radio series gradually premiering on Resonance Extra. He is also half of noise duo enn{kdog, along with Mysterious House.
