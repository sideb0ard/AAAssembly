Algorithmic Art Assembly
March 10–11–12 2022
Gray Area, San Francisco

RM Francis

RM Francis is a Seattle-based artist working with computer-generated sound. Since 2011 his work has been released on nada, CO-DEPENDENT, Conditional, and ETAT, among other labels. His most recent work, Every Single Person Has Some Muscle for six synthetic voices, will be released on FLEA in spring 2022.
